Leader

Architecting inclusive growth at population scale. Former CSRO at TCS, advancing economic mobility for 30M+ people across 55 countries.

Partners in Purpose

Balaji is a C-level executive with over 26 years of U.S. and global experience, building purposeful organizations and stewarding inclusive growth.

As Chief Social Responsibility Officer at Tata Consultancy Services, he envisioned and led the $30 billion tech firm's strategic corporate philanthropic efforts, leveraging public, private, and nonprofit partnerships for large-scale social impact. His purpose ecosystem model resulted in 20M volunteering hours, $40M in pro bono value, 188 partnerships, and collective wisdom of 11,000 cross-sector leaders to support policy and advocacy.

"My mission is to harness the power of purpose, people and technology to advance access, equity and inclusion for all across society."

Advisor

Trusted by

Balaji has advised CXOs and Fortune 500 companies, supporting over $30 billion digital transformation deals, and leading 200+ complex, global change management efforts and M&As.

A firm believer in the power of building movements and enabling systems change, he has advised numerous U.S., Indian, Asian and global organizations to aspire for big bets, ground in their values, innovate their strategy, and elevate their impact.

A Charles H. Moore 'Charlie Award' winner, Balaji's extensive board and advisory service includes the World Economic Forum, U.S. Chamber of Commerce Foundation, Chief Executives for Corporate Purpose, Women Business Collaborative, and Commission on Asian Philanthropy.

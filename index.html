Balaji is a C-level executive with over 25 years of U.S. and global experience, building purposeful organizations and stewarding inclusive growth. Guided by a belief in "prosperity at the last mile," he architected one of the world's largest impact portfolios at the $30B technology firm Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), advancing economic mobility for 30+ million women, youth, and marginalized groups across 55 countries.

Mobilizing 188 partners, 11K cross-sector leaders, 20M volunteer hours, $40M in pro bono value and $650M in community investments, he drove groundbreaking technology innovations in education, workforce development, digital inclusion, health, sustainability, and social entrepreneurship.

Advisor

Trusted Advisor to Fortune 500 Companies & Foundations

Balaji has advised CXOs and Fortune 500 companies, supporting over $30 billion digital transformation deals, and leading 200+ complex, global change management efforts and M&As.

A firm believer in the power of building movements & enabling systems change, he has advised numerous impact U.S., Indian, Asian and global organizations to aspire for big bets, ground in their values, innovate their strategy & elevate their impact.

A Charles H. Moore 'Charlie Award' winner, Balaji's extensive board and advisory service include the World Economic Forum, U.S. Chamber of Commerce Foundation, Chief Executives for Corporate Purpose, Women Business Collaborative and Commission on Asian Philanthropy.

Passionate about Asia's promise as a model for the world, he serves as a Board Director of a $30M impact fund fostering livelihoods for the next billion 'aspirants', and a strategic advisor to corporates, foundations, and nonprofits.

Keynote Topics

Leading with purpose in the era of stakeholder capitalism. Explore how purpose-driven organizations outperform peers, attract top talent, and build stronger brands while driving sustainable growth.
Building and scaling world-class social impact programs. Learn strategies for mobilizing partnerships, measuring outcomes, and creating lasting change across communities and countries.
Leveraging artificial intelligence for positive social change. Discover how AI can address global challenges in education, healthcare, workforce development, and sustainable development.
Creating inclusive workplaces in the digital transformation era. Explore strategies for building diverse teams, fostering belonging, and preparing the workforce for emerging opportunities.
Integrating sustainability into core business strategy. Learn how to build resilient, regenerative business models that create value for all stakeholders while addressing environmental and social challenges.
